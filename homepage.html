Welcome to GreenStride!

Our platform allows you to calculate your carbon footprint based on daily activities across several key categories. Here's a brief overview of how each category contributes to the calculation:

Food Category

This category focuses on your dietary choices. Subcategories include:

  • Eating In: Calculates emissions based on the type and quantity of meat and carbohydrates consumed at home.
  • Eating Out: Considers the environmental impact of meals consumed outside the home, including type and frequency of meals.

Energy Category

Evaluates the carbon emissions from your energy consumption. Subcategories cover different energy sources such as:

  • Grid Electricity: The common electricity supply.
  • Solar Energy: Usage of solar panels.
  • Gas: Includes both heating and cooking gas consumption.

Transportation Category

Assesses the impact of your travel choices. Subcategories include:

  • Electric Vehicles: Covers electric cars, scooters, and bikes.
  • Diesel and Gasoline Vehicles: Includes cars, motorcycles, and scooters using fossil fuels.
  • Public Transport: The environmental footprint of using public transit systems.

Waste Management Category

Focuses on how you dispose of waste, with specific attention to recycling methods. Subcategories include:

  • Plastics: Options for sorting and recycling or incineration of plastic waste.
  • Metals: The recycling or disposal methods for metal wastes.

Each category feeds data into our carbon emission calculation model, which utilizes API data to generate precise emission estimates based on your entries.
